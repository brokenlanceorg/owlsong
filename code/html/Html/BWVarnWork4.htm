Introspection

The fallacy by which most human beings rely on, to their detriment, is the belief that we have no choice but to become what our society encourages. Human intervention naturally creates a shallow framework upon which each individual of humankind builds the structure of his life. It is this framework which most people perceive when viewing themselves. By viewing their character as simplistic and lacking in depth, most human beings believe that everything they do must continue to be without substance in order to supply the needs and desires of their very existence. By viewing themselves in this light, the vast majority have failed in the primary purpose of introspection.
