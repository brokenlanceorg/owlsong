This month's mp3 is actually two different versions of the same song.

The first mp3 is essentially a poetry reading against background music, while the second mp3 is my re-arranged guitar piece of the original song, (written by me and Varn).

Times Long Gone

Trees larger than life
Soft driven strife
The hilltop in the distance
Recalls a familiar tune
As trees grow and flowers bloom
But something special covers my eyes
As hope propspers and people die
Tunnels barrow into the souls
Of mortal men that we all know
But times of old are all long gone
And places I've loved are in my song
Looking at the places I've been
Wondering if I'll see you again
I know that it's been said:
"Everyday people have got into your head"

Sunlit fields of green
My eyes they see everything
But I see the sun still shines
Upon far off places and fields sublime
