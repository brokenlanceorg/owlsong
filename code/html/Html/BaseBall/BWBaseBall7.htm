Jaro eyes us menacingly as he walks down the bleachers. "I'll git you," he says to the three of us, but to no one in particular.

"You alright Morgan?" asks Varn.

"Yeah, I think so," he responds while rubbing a dark red spot on his neck.

"Your shirt is torn," I point out.

"My mom's gonna kill me," answers Morgan.

"Damnit, I hate those rednecks," growls Varn.

I silently stare at the stadium light reflected off the bleachers contemplating how we can revenge ourselves. I can feel my muscles shaking from all the adrenaline.

Then Roy says in a solemn voice: "Boys, don't you worry about them. Some day soon, you'll be the ones in control." With that, Roy walks down the bleachers and off into the crowd.

"What the hell does that mean?" asks Morgan, still rubbing his neck.

"I don't know, but I need something to eat. Do you guys want anything?" I say and start to head off to the concession stand.

"No, I can't eat anything now," says Varn.

"Me neither," agrees Morgan.

I walk down to buy some sunflower seeds and a Mountain Dew, and try to calm down by watching the kids play cup-ball.

I can hear the 'ping' of an aluminum bat and then I hear someone say "Heads up!" The batter sends a foul ball overhead and into the parking lot. All the kids instantly quit the cup-ball game and race after the foul ball. (Whoever turns in a foul ball to the concession stand gets a free large drink of their choice.) I followed the kids into the parking lot to see who will emerge the champion─the loner I spotted earlier has a jump-start on everyone. Just when I thought he was about to grab the ball, the wind gets knocked out of me and I feel a clenched fist bury itself in my stomach. I dropped to my knees grasping for breath; I feel as if I'm going to throw up. My vision blurs, but I can make out the sunflower seeds mixed in with the yellow Mountain Dew on the pavement.
