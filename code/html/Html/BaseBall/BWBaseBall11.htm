Ironically, we ended up playing each other in the regional finals at the end of the summer. It was bittersweet; Varn and I fought so hard to get to the All-Star team only to face each other as opponents in the finals. It was another bit of irony when Chuck Holt, the hitless boy wonder, the son of a used tire salesman turned politician, broke the tie with a homer over the left-center fence in the eleventh inning.

I remember being furiously angry after that game─I've never been that angry over a loss since. But, as angry and upset as I was after that game, the tableau that's still burned into my mind is pudgy Morgan, with tears in his eyes, standing alone by the third-base fence on the night of the All-Star announcements.
