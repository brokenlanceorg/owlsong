One point to note is that there is no explicit quantization in the derivation, rather it is predicted by solving (8) for a particular wavefunction given a potential and energy. The meaning is y is not at all obvious. For a classical wavefunction, the value of y is the vertical displacement from some equilibrium position. For the quantum-mechanical wavefunction, the interpretation was given by the German physicist Max Born in 1926. Since y is usually a complex function, multiplying y by its complex conjugate gives a real number.
