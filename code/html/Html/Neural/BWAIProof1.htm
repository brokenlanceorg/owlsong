The following proof demonstrates that the human mind, as understood today, is logically equivalent to a Universal Turing Machine (UTM). It immediately follows that since modern programmable computers are UTMs, a computer can be sufficiently programmed to emulate the human mind.

It is well known in cognitive science that artificial neural networks capture every salient feature of the physical workings of the human (and consequently other life form’s) brain.
