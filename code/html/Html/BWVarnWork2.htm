Is it lack of wisdom or a quenching of a thirst?
It doesn’t really matter for neither one is worse
Whatever in this shoebox town keeps people bound in chains
draws strength from every victim and enjoys inflicting pain.

It likes to treat each person as a means to an evil end
Sucking blood from victims and dragging them to its den.
There’s only one way to avoid the venom from its spit
Don’t get lured to its lair and avoid ever being bit.
