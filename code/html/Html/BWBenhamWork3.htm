This mp3's heart and soul is the drum track, which I ripped off Black Sabbath's self-titled debut album. For those of you familiar with Sabbath, you'll note that this is the track 'Wall of Sleep' (hence the name of this mp3). I was listening to those drums one day and I came up with the riffs for this song.

Although I don't know for sure, it's rather certain that the title 'Wall of Sleep' owes its namesake to the eminent horror storyteller H.P. Lovecraft whose tale, by the same name, is rather reminiscent of certain backwoods cultures.
