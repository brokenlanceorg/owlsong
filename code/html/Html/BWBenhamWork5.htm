Here we have yet another J.R.R. Tolkien inspired song. This song is named after the wise old wizard, or more appropriatly an Istari, from the trilogy. This wise one was known as 'Mithrandir' in the more popular Elven tongues, and in the speech of Men, he was known as 'Gandalf'. The song tries to reflect the prodigious prowess that Gandalf commanded.

Those Van Halen fans out there should recognize the drums, as they have been ripped off 'Intruder' from the 'Diver Down' album. The song is completely in 'E' harmonic minor.
