Satriani is, by far, one of the most talented guitarists to ever play the six-string. 'Tears' is his only piece that I can play all the way through.

I added the volume swells to the track because they seemed so effective on 'Goodbye Blue Sky', but in this case, I'm afraid they may take away from the beautiful finger-picked arpeggios underneath.
