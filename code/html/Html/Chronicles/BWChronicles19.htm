“Yes, I remember that talisman now,” chimed in Benham, “but, as with you Conduff, I just can’t figure out the meaning of those segments…it must be some personal meaning…okay Dossette, we give up, what do they mean?”

“Well,” began Dossette timidly, “I think you guys are on the wrong track, my symbol is this.” Dossette held out his hand forming the surfer “hang-loose” sign, which consisted of a clenched fist with extended thumb and pinkie finger.

Varn groaned as if he had just been hit in the stomach, while Conduff just shook his head in disappointment. Benham looked away toward the wall and slowly shook his head saying, “Man, that’s just not gonna work, that’s just not gonna work at all.”

They tried to make the best of it, but when it came time for the sunset ritual, they couldn’t find the Led Zeppelin IV tape, and the sunset just didn’t have the same impact as when “Stairway to Heaven” was sweetening the air. Disconcerted, they decided to call it a night and ended the meeting early.
