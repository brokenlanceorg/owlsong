Introspection

This framework of our existence created by human intervention is nothing more than a cocoon. The true “self” is inside the cocoon and longing for us to set it free. The person who allows society to shape him into the self-centered cut-throat specialist of human desire is the person who remains trapped in the cocoon. But he who bursts free from the restraints of the cocoon and finds his true potential is metamorphasized into a new creature, a stronger creature, a tragic hero.
